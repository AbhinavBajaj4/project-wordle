Instructions

Wordle, a simple word game, is a fantastic brainstorming tool


Wordle is a web-based word game created and developed by Welsh software engineer Josh Wardle


RULES


  • You have to guess the Wordle in six goes or less.

  • Every word you enter must be in the word list.That hasn't been disclosed, but presumably it's based on a dictionary.

  • A correct letter turns green.

  • A correct letter in the wrong place turns yellow.

  • An incorrect letter turns gray.

  • Letters can be used more than once
